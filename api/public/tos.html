revision 1.0.4 // standard business agreement

01. terminal_access

by establishing a connection to sentinelpay security nodes, you acknowledge and agree to comply with the operational protocols outlined in this document. unauthorized access attempts or exploitation of api infrastructure will result in immediate termination of service and permanent blacklisting of associated wallet addresses.

02. service_integrity

sentinelpay provides high-fidelity risk analysis through its proprietary scoring engine. while we strive for 100% precision, all outputs are probabilistic security assessments. the service is provided on an "as-available" basis. we do not guarantee uninterrupted node connectivity during high-load periods or network upgrades.

03. operational_limits

users are prohibited from executing the following sequences:

  • reverse-engineering scoring weights or private security heuristics.
  • flood-attacks or any method designed to disrupt api response latency.
  • using the scanner to facilitate fraudulent or malicious smart contract deployments.
  • multiplexing api keys across unauthorized enterprise environments.

04. credit_economics

all transactions involving sentinelpay credits are final. credits represent prepayed compute resources and hold no cash value. refunds are only processed in cases of documented system-wide failure exceeding 72 hours of downtime, at the sole discretion of sentinelpay core.

05. liability_shield

sentinelpay and its developers shall not be liable for any financial impact, lost assets, or smart contract failures resulting from the use or misuse of our scoring data. you are solely responsible for verifying transaction security before signing any data with your private keys.

06. data_privacy

we adhere to a zero-knowledge data retention policy for public scanners. for registered business clients, we collect only the essential metadata required for billing and security auditing. we never sell or distribute client data to third-party analytics firms.

07. jurisdictional_law

this agreement is governed by the laws of the republic of croatia. any disputes arising from the use of sentinelpay services shall be resolved within the competent court system of croatia, following standard european commercial protocols.
