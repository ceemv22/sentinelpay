privacy policy

last updated: may 03, 2026

01. data philosophy

at sentinelpay, we operate on a principle of zero-knowledge retention for public users and radical data minimization for our business partners. we believe security should never come at the cost of your digital sovereignty.

02. information we collect

we collect only the essential data points required to maintain infrastructure stability and prevent systemic abuse:

  • technical logs: truncated ip addresses and user-agent strings for rate-limiting purposes.
  • account data: email address and cryptographic session identifiers for registered portal users.
  • transaction metadata: public blockchain addresses submitted for risk scoring. we do not link these to your personal identity.

03. use of data

your data is exclusively used to provide the requested security analytics. we do not utilize your submitted wallet addresses for marketing purposes, nor do we build behavioral profiles of our users.

04. cookie policy

sentinelpay utilizes minimal functional cookies required for session management and security verification (via cloudflare turnstile). we do not use third-party tracking pixels or advertising cookies.

05. third-party services

we utilize cloudflare for ddos protection and supabase for secure authentication. these providers are compliant with global data protection standards (gdpr). no transaction-specific data is shared with these entities.

06. data retention

public scan history is ephemeral and automatically purged from our caching layer. registered business data is retained only for the duration of the active subscription period.

07. user rights

under gdpr and international privacy laws, you have the right to access, rectify, or erase any personal data held by sentinelpay. requests can be directed to our security officer through the portal's support channels.

08. security measures

all internal data transit is encrypted using tls 1.3. sensitive account identifiers are hashed using industry-standard cryptographic algorithms to ensure your data remains secure in the event of an infrastructure breach.

09. updates to policy

we reserve the right to update this policy as our infrastructure evolves. significant changes will be broadcast through the sentinelpay dashboard and official communication channels.
