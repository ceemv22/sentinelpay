## 3. vesting schedule (the "s-tier protection")

to ensure long-term commitment, the equity will be earned over time (vested) based on the following schedule:

- **1-year cliff**: no equity is earned until the 12-month mark of active service.
- **monthly vesting**: after the 1-year cliff, equity vests in equal monthly increments (1/48th per month) over the following 36 months (4 years total).
- **termination**: if service is terminated (voluntarily or involuntarily) before the 1-year cliff, zero equity is granted.
