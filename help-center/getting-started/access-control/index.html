access control

sentinelpay provides access controls to manage permissions within your organization.

each organization member is assigned one of two roles:

  • owner: the user who created the organization. has full access to all settings, team management, and api key operations.
  • developer: the default role for invited members. can make api requests and view scan history within the organization.

when you create an organization, you are automatically assigned as the owner. any members you invite will be assigned the developer role.

managing members

to invite a collaborator, navigate to the team tab in your organization dashboard and click invite member. enter the recipient's email address and send the invite. invitation links expire after 7 days.

transferring ownership

each sentinelpay organization must have exactly one owner. to transfer ownership, contact us at support@sentinelpay.org with your organization details.
