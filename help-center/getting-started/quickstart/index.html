quickstart

get from zero to your first risk score in under five minutes.

step 1 — create your account

go to sentinelpay.org and register with your email address. you will receive a verification email — click the link inside to activate your account. once verified, log in to access the dashboard.

step 2 — create an organization

after logging in for the first time you will be prompted to create your first organization. enter a display name and a unique slug (e.g. acme-corp). the slug becomes part of your organization's identifier and cannot be changed after creation.

select a plan during setup. all new organizations receive 10 free credits — enough to run your first scans immediately without entering payment details. you can add a payment method later when you need more credits.

step 3 — find your api key

your api key is automatically provisioned when your organization is created. it is displayed in full exactly once — copy it and store it securely in your environment's secrets manager or .env file. after the initial display, only the last four characters remain visible in the dashboard as a reference.

if you miss the key, the organization owner can roll it from the settings tab. rolling issues a new key and immediately invalidates the old one.

step 4 — make your first scan

send a POST request to the scan endpoint with the wallet address you want to assess:

curl -X POST https://sentinelpay.org/v1/score \
  -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
  -H "x-api-key: YOUR_API_KEY" \
  -d '{"wallet": "0xAbCd1234..."}'

a successful response returns http 200 with a json body:

{
  "score": 20,
  "category": "low",
  "flags": ["new_wallet"],
  "history_incomplete": false,
  "timestamp": "2026-06-01T10:00:00.000Z"
}

the score is an integer from 0 to 100. category is one of low, medium, or high. flags lists every heuristic that fired. see understanding heuristics for full scoring logic.
