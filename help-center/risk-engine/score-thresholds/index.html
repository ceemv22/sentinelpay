score thresholds

interpret risk scores and set thresholds that align with your platform's risk appetite.

recommended tiers

sentinelpay returns a raw integer score from 0 to 100. we provide three baseline categories to help you decide how to act on each result:

low 0 — 29

safe for automatic processing. low probability of illicit activity.

medium 30 — 59

some suspicious signals detected. manual review or secondary aml checks recommended.

high 60 — 100

direct links to high-risk entities. blocking these transactions is recommended.

score 100 — sanctioned entity

a score of exactly 100 with the sanctioned_entity flag means the wallet address was found directly in our blocklist. this is the most severe classification — no further analysis is performed and the block recommendation is absolute.

setting your own threshold

the recommended thresholds above are starting points. the score field is a raw integer, so you can apply any custom threshold logic in your own backend. depending on your platform's risk appetite, you may choose to block any score above 40, or to allow medium-risk deposits with enhanced kyc verification.

the flags array gives you granular signal data. even at the same score, a wallet flagged for mixer_interaction carries a different risk profile than one flagged only for new_wallet.
