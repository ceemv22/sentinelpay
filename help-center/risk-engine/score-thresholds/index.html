score thresholds

recommended tiers

we provide three baseline categories based on the numerical score:

  • 0 – 30 (low) — low probability of illicit activity. safe for automatic processing.
  • 31 – 69 (medium) — some suspicious signals. we recommend manual review or secondary aml checks.
  • 70 – 100 (high) — direct links to high-risk entities. we recommend blocking these transactions.
