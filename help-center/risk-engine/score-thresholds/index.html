score thresholds

interpret risk scores and set thresholds that align with your platform's risk appetite.

recommended tiers

we provide three baseline categories based on the numerical score:

  • 0 — 29 (low) — low probability of illicit activity. safe for automatic processing.
  • 30 — 59 (medium) — some suspicious signals detected. we recommend manual review or secondary aml checks.
  • 60 — 100 (high) — direct links to high-risk entities. we recommend blocking these transactions.
